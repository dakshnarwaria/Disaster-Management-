Disaster Preparedness Modules

Engaging, interactive modules to educate and prepare you for various disaster scenarios.

Earthquake Preparedness

Learn how to prepare for and respond to earthquakes. This module covers safety measures before, during, and after an earthquake, including securing your home, identifying safe spots, and first aid basics.

Flood Safety

Understand the risks associated with floods and how to stay safe. This module includes information on flood warnings, evacuation procedures, and post-flood recovery.

Fire Safety

Learn essential fire safety tips, including how to prevent fires, what to do in case of a fire, and how to use a fire extinguisher. This module also covers evacuation plans and meeting points.

Severe Weather Preparedness

Prepare for severe weather conditions such as hurricanes, tornadoes, and thunderstorms. This module covers how to monitor weather forecasts, prepare emergency kits, and seek shelter.
